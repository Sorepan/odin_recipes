Panna Cotta

Photo by salsachica on Freeimages.com

Description

Classic Italian dessert that's easy to make and delicious to eat.

Ingredients

  • 250g heavy cream
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla extract.
  2. Pour the cream mixture into a saucepan and cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the cream reaches 85Â°C.
  3. Remove the cream from the heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  4. Once the cream is cooled, pour it into individual molds or a single container. If desired, add a tablespoon of melted butter on top.
  5. Refrigerate the panna cotta for at least 4 hours. Serve chilled, garnished with fresh fruit or fruit sauce if desired.
