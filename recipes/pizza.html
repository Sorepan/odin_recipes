Pizza

Description

A pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs, then baked until crispy and flavorful. It’s loved worldwide for its versatility and delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 packet (7g) dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes until foamy. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead until smooth.
  2. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  3. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Roll the dough into a round shape and place it on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella, and add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Slice and enjoy!
