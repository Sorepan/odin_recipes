Enchiladas

Photo by DΛVΞ GΛRCIΛ

Description

An enchilada is a traditional Mexican dish made of a corn tortilla rolled around a filling (commonly chicken, beef, cheese, or beans) and covered with a savory chili sauce. It’s baked until hot and flavorful, often topped with cheese or fresh garnishes.

Ingredients

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • ½ cup shredded cheese
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and lightly fry each tortilla for a few seconds per side to make them pliable.
  2. Place a small amount of chicken (or cheese) in the center of each tortilla.
  3. Roll them up and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.
  4. Pour enchilada sauce evenly over the rolled tortillas, then sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve hot.
